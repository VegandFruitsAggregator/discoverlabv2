🔬 This Experiment
What we are doing: Releasing a concentrated substance and measuring how uniformly it spreads.
Our aim: Find what controls the speed and completeness of diffusion.
What we will find: A formula that predicts concentration uniformity with great accuracy.
🌸
Think of perfume spreading in a room. Spray it in one corner and wait — it spreads everywhere without anyone moving air. Higher temperature = faster spread. Diffusion works the same way in every living cell.
The Formula
CONCENTRATION UNIFORMITY
U = D · (C₀/100) · (1 + 0.02·ΔT) · t
U = uniformity (%)D = diffusion rateC₀ = initial conc.t = time (s)
